General Information about the Program


The program offers Bachelor graduates in Industrial Engineering an internationally-oriented, generalist qualification.

Broad Interdisciplinary Offerings

In addition to a well-coordinated curriculum, students can select from a wide range of elective modules, allowing them to tailor their studies through exciting projects. Sharpen your interdisciplinary profile by choosing from the content of the Master's programs offered by our three faculties: Engineering, Business and Law, and Design!

Practical and Personalized

You will learn in small groups, prepare seminars and workshops, and engage in practice-oriented project work. This approach ensures you achieve an excellent level of professional training, tailored to meet the current demands of companies. It prepares you for positions in product management, procurement, project management, production, or controlling.

International Orientation

Depending on the individual selection of courses, the second semester can largely be completed in English—an ideal preparation for roles in an international context.
